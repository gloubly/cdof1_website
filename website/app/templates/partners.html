Hear & Know

Hear & Know is a startup created in 2012 specialized in Internet of Things and connected objects.
The company focuses on security and tracking for valuable assets, offering innovative "GPS of things" technology that works seamlessly both indoors and outdoors.
Their first applications target the protection of individuals in high-risk environments and the precise tracking of vulnerable individuals.
