Gas Detection

Our team is working on a project u3lizing Arduino modules to develop a gas detection system.
The key components provided for this project include:

  • GPS Modules: These are used to pinpoint the loca3on of the detected gases, crucial for mapping the source and area affected.
  • Gas Sensors: Specifically designed to detect various gases such as methane, LPG, and other gases of interest, depending on our chosen configuration.
  • Arduino Chip: Serves as the core processing unit for interfacing the GPS and gas sensors.

Project Purpose

We aim to detect and identify abnormal concentrations of gases, partcularly those emanating from mining actvities. This involves:

  • Determining the specific gases we aim to detect.
  • Programming the gas sensor to identify target gases.
  • Using the GPS module to log the exact location of detection.

Data Management

All collected data will be submited to a centralized database, enabling detailed analysis and monitoring of gas emissions over time.

Next Steps

  • Finalize the list of gases to detect.
  • Calibrate the gas sensor to ensure accuracy.
  • Integrate the system and test in real-world conditions.
