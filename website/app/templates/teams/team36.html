Team 36

Ammunition recognition

The primary objective of this project is to develop a comprehensive system capable of accurately identifying and categorizing various types of mines and munitions using artificial intelligence and advanced image processing techniques.
Through the deployment of machine learning algorithms and automated image recognition models, we aim to create a solution that can reliably detect and classify hazardous materials based on their visual characteristics. This technology will significantly enhance safety by providing field operatives, demining teams, and defense personnel with quick, precise identification of explosive threats.
The system will be trained on a diverse dataset of imagery to ensure robustness and adaptability across different environments and scenarios. By leveraging deep learning, the model will progressively improve its recognition accuracy, even under challenging conditions.
This project aspires to make substantial contributions to humanitarian and military efforts, reducing the risks associated with unidentified explosive devices while streamlining the identification process.
